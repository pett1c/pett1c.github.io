derealisation / дереализация

this is my absolutely first serious release on all platforms, my first experience of working with distribution, and in general my first experience of working with really normal sounding music. previously i worked only with full experimentalism, and in no way even thought about going to platforms. but with the first track (which is also the first in the album) i realized that i need to go to platforms, that's for sure. the album as a whole turned out to be quite melodic, and a lot of people loved the last track in particular. i also think that this track was my best in terms of processing, and it's also the longest track on the album, which i'm proud of.

„Manchmal muss man eine Idee leben, um sie in die Welt zu bringen.“
